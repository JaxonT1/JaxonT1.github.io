Black Box Project

In our New Media and Tech class we are working on finding what’s inside the black box. The black box is a box that we put water into and water comes out. It comes out of the box very inconsistently and we cannot see inside of the box. We are trying to figure out how the water comes out. We have taken data and created drafts of the black box in new media and tech. We then created real life models out of cardboard and other classroom materials. We then created 3D models in maya and animated it. Our goal was to fully animate and render our theory of what was inside the black box.

Claim

I think that there is a tube that siphons the water in the container when it fills to a certain point. The most important data is how inconsistently the water would come out of the black box. During our data taking stages we took data as a class and the data that we received varied. For example, trial one, we input 100ml and 160ml came out. Trial four, we input 1000ml and 1600ml came out. After we took data on the black box we started creating the real life models. We used things like cardboard and plastic tubes to replicate our theories of what is inside the black box. I used a container for the water to fall into from the funnel and put a tube inside for the siphon effect. After we finished creating the real life models we tested them and took data. We kept data of how much water we input and how much water would come out of the model. When we tested the model the water that we input would come out of the box very inconsistently. At sometimes the water would come out in very small amounts, in other instances the entire box would empty.
