Jaxon Taylor's Screenprinting Project

In this project I learned how to utilize photoshop and how to print what I made in photoshop and make a screenprint. I learned many skills in photoshop like how to manipulate text, how to create halftones, and how to manipulate images. I also learned how to use other adobe apps like premier. Premier helped me to create video projects. I learned how to voice over video of my B roll creating this project and helped me make a smooth looking video.

Growth In A 21st Century Skill

The 21st century skill that I showed the most growth was creativity and innovation. I grew through my creativity through coming up with a cause I would like to represent through art. I was able to take pictures and create a powerful meaning behind a few words.

Materials

  • Wood
  • Woodsaw
  • Wood glue
  • Nail gun
  • Mesh
  • Plastic print paper
